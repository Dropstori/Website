who we are

At our core, we are storytellers. We have an overwhelming ability to communicate in the most relatable fashion, the intricate details about the beauty and uniqueness of everyone we encounter. Our wording of events and hope, positions the people who trust us for untold success.
In the hands of job seekers, what we offer is a chance to make their journey to their next employment opportunity a believable tale of hard work and dedication to shared values. We help them, connect with recruiters and show them a path to the words that highlight their uniqueness.
For every entrepreneur, we are the unsung, unheard, and yet unmatched competitive advantage. We are the reason they walk into a room, confident of their ability to convince the investor or would be customer of the value of their product. We help them make pitch decks that cannot be ignored, and phrase enticing value propositions.
We are the agency of choice for anyone who nurses thoughts of an easy access to opportunity. We achieve our mission to use storytelling as a bridge to access opportunities in the world markets, by teaching groups in schools or after school, and by consulting for individuals who come to us.

why we do it

We believe everyone is not cut out to be an entrepreneur, and financial freedom can also be achieved by working a nine to five. We thus help people find employment opportunities that reward and value their commitment and effort. Trusting that the livelihoods of the people we work with and the ones they care dearly for, will be improves if they tell compelling naratives about why they want the job and why they are perfect candidates.
Our drive comes from knowing that schools are not set up to teach people how to job hunt. Students are taught to master the skills needed for the job, and school assumes finding the job can be figured out independently. Yet experience counters this, and people infact need more help than we have fashioned our schools to give. What we do, we do for the sole purpose of filling this gap, the gap between skill and the opportunity to use it.
For the entrepreneurs, the ones with the courage to embrace the beauty and uncertainty that running your venture presents, we provide a service that helps them access more financing, and when it serves them, we help them pitch to customers as well.
Our desire is to see that entrepreneurs can communicate passionately, the value they provide. We a keen on helping them bring aboard the people with access to funds they need, with much more ease, and eloquence. The kind of ease only a good and unforgettable story can guarantee.

what matters

As an organization, our focus is on people. Helping them preserve their dignity, while navigating the challenges of job hunting is our number one priority. Make no mistake, we want to be profitable, but our profit must come from job seekers and entrepreneurs living their dreams. If we were not so inlove with storytelling, we would have been a happiness agency. A place of hope for people who struggle with finding and gaining rights to the finest opportunities their skills deserve, those for which your heart beats.
We cherish creating memories with the people we work with, seeing the joy that comes with earning more, and knowing we contributed in all that matters to us. On most days, we are not telling stories, we are uncovering layers of beauty, from the strangers we call friends. For us, it is not enough that we help you gain access to opportunity, but that we build a friendly space for you to uncover your unique value proposition which you can leverage to navigate the rest of your career.

when we commit.

Forevery client or client group we work with, we take on the problems of the group with the conviction that for all who put in the work, there is only one outcome and we do not rest until we get them to where they belong. Thats why we have world class client follow-up schemes, focusing on providing the support needed to stay motivated in the job hunt.
Unlike most agencies, we do not promise heaven here on earth, we do not as much make you feel it will be effortless. Our truth, and our bond, is that we can get this done with easier than you can without us. We also share that, should poor storytelling and interview nerves be one of your night mares, then you will sleep easy every night from the first one you work with us.
